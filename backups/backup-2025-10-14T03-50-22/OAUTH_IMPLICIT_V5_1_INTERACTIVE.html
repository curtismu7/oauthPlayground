When to Use Implicit Flow

OAuth Implicit Flow is designed for public clients (like SPAs) that cannot securely store a client secret. It's perfect for single-page applications and mobile apps.

What is an Authorization Request?

An authorization request redirects users to PingOne's authorization server where they authenticate and consent to sharing their information with your application. This is the first step in obtaining tokens.
